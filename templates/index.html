Interactive Data Science Project Exploratory Analysis Byte Example

This is the Exploratory Analysis Byte of the Interactive Data Science class.

Andrew ID: shenghuj

Question for Exploring

How is my living pattern looks like?

Did I spend all my time on homework and limit my life only in campus and home and live in an unhealthy way?




Visualization 1

This vissuliaztion shows the my GPS and GSM network location records from late March to mid April by android AWARENESS APP. From this map, we could see that the most frequent places I visited includes, no surprise, Home, Campus and Studio. But restaurants along Murray and N Craig, Supermarket like Target and Trade Joe and Chinese Market are also very frequent palce I visited. Interestingly enough, I did not realize that I actually go to these market so frequently.

Moreover, as one my undergraduate classmate came to pittsburgh for a conference and was accommodated in hotel in downtown, I also go to downtown several times for greeting him. So, overall, I find that I actually was not that obsessed in campus and home.




Visualization 2

This visualization shows the Activity mode I have been in and recorded by the activity recognition plug-in of AWARE Framework APP. As these recognition are made by some algorithms based on the reading of accelerometer and other sensors, they will not be fully accuratue. For example, in this bar chart, we could find that there is a big part named "tilting". I would assume that it maybe because I was on bus and downhilling the Forbs Ave. In the data provided by the recognition plug-in, there is a column contains "confidence" infomation. In this visualization, I clearn the data by filter out the data with confidence lower than 50 to get rid of the "unknow" records from visualization in default.




Visualization 3

This visualization shows how frequently I actvatie my cellphone in different time slot. From this visualization, I could found that the slightly-after-noon time is my most active time on cellphone. And from this visualization, as the cellphone usually not active before 12pm, I will consider that this means that I usually wake up after that time in this period.




Conclutions and Learnings

From these visualizations of some figures of my life style infomation, I found that actually I was not in a very bad living style. I actually spent a big part of time on things beside living between Campus and Home. However, as I have to finish assignments one many late night, I wake very late. I should try to wake up a bit early for being a more morning person.
